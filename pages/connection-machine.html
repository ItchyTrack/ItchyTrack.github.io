Connection Machine

What is Connection Machine?

Connection Machine is an open-source and cross-platform desktop application for designing and simulating digital circuits at scale. It's built as a university project for RCOS. You can get a build of the application from the releases page on GitHub, or build it yourself.

Features

  • Tensor connect: Perform enormous connections by projecting your circuit across space.
  • Fast simulation: Custom-built engine can simulate entire computers in real-time.
  • Open-source: Free to use and contribute to on GitHub.
  • Custom components: Create and reuse your own components.

Planned Features

  • GPU acceleration: Perform simulations at blazing speeds.
  • Real-world interaction: Connect designs to real devices for sound or game input.
  • Online circuit catalog: Share and download designs with the community.
